### General Soldering Training

General soldering training will cover operation of the soldering iron and how it works, what tools to use when fixing soldering mistakes, and how to clean up the workspace after oneself.

This training will not cover basic soldering techniques (techniques for soldering a functional PCB with wide variety of components). It is highly recomended you sign up for basic soldering after completion of general training.

#### Objectives
After completing general soldering training, one can:
- Possess the skills to confidently operate a soldering iron while adhering to safety guidelines.
- Understand and execute basic soldering techniques for soldering standard resistors on a PCB and wire joints.
- Exhibit proper lab etiquette, including maintaining a clean and organized workspace.
